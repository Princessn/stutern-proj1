Name of food: Creamy Chicke Pasta

Nutritional Facts: 1 cup: 431 calories, 25g fat (14g saturated fat), 121mg cholesterol, 420mg sodium, 27g carbohydrate (3g sugars, 2g fiber), 23g protein.

Creamy pasta: Creamy pasta like many pasta dish originates from Spain, it made up of high calories and fats. Many people around the world enjoy this dish as it is simple and tasty


S/N Ingredients Measurements
1 Pasta 2 Cups
2 Sliced mushrooms 2 cups
3 sliced green onion 1 cup
4 butter 2 table spoon
5 white wine half a cup
6 minced garlic 1 table spoon
7 flour 1 table spoon
8 water 1/3 cup
9 heavy whipping cream 1 cup
10 cooked chicken Any quantity
11 capers, drained 2 table spoon
12 salt and black paper 1/4 teaspoon each

Directions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, saute mushrooms and onions in butter for 4-5 minutes or until tender. Add wine and garlic. Bring to a boil; cook until liquid is reduced by half, about 5 minutes.
  2. Combine flour and water until smooth; gradually add to mushroom mixture. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Stir in cream. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 4-5 minutes or until heated through.
  3. Add the pasta, chicken, capers, salt and pepper to cream sauce. Cook for 3-4 minutes or until heated through. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
